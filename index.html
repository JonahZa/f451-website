About Us

This section is just a short paragraph about the details of the creation of this project.

Our Mission

This project was originally just created for an English project. However, it quickly morphed into a much larger idea.

This would be the first movie adaptation of Fahrenheit 451 that would truly show Bradbury's message to the public. However, why is this necessary? We believe that now, more than ever, Fahrenheit 451 is needed to show the dangers of technology and censorship of information.

Therefore, the movie will be the closest adaptation of Bradbury's original vision, aiming to show the reality of the censorship of information and the use of technology today.
